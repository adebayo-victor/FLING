About the Seminar

Road safety is a shared responsibility. This seminar is specifically designed for motor park users – drivers, conductors, passengers, and park officials – to foster a deeper understanding of road regulations, defensive driving techniques, and the importance of vehicle maintenance.

Our expert speakers will cover topics ranging from traffic laws and sign interpretations to first aid and conflict resolution within public transport settings. Join us to contribute to safer roads and more organized motor parks for everyone.

Key Topics & What You'll Learn

Understanding Warning Signs

Learn to identify and react to crucial warning signs, anticipating hazards and navigating safely.

Adhering to Prohibitory Rules

Master the importance of 'No Entry', 'Stop', and other prohibitory signs for safe and compliant movement.

Interpreting Informational Signs

Navigate unfamiliar routes with ease by understanding directional and informational signage for efficient travel.

❤️

First Aid & Emergency Response

Gain essential skills for immediate and effective response in case of road accidents or medical emergencies.
